Research

My MS Project focused on the following problem statement: given a multi-view dataset, how can we synthesize multi-view sequences that are consistent across multiple robots. This aligns with my long-term research goal to create intelligent systems with world models that exhibit spatial intelligence - the ability to interpret, model, and generate 3D representations of the physical world.
